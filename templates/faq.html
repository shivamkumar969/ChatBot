FAQs

Is SMS Varanasi private or Government?

SMS Varanasi is a private institute that was established in the year 1995 and has been granted Autonomous Status by UGC and ‘A” Grade by NAAC.

Is SMS Varanasi good for MBA?

School of Management Sciences Varanasi offers one of the most reputed MBA programs in the whole of North India. This year, it invited more than 50 Marketing, Finance, HR and IT companies to its college campus – especially for MBA & MCA students. Hence, it stands out for providing better placement opportunities than other MBA Colleges in UP.

Is admission open at SMS College Varanasi?

Admissions for postgraduate courses like MBA at SMS Varanasi typically begin early in the year and are based on scores from national entrance exams like CAT, MAT, XAT, CMAT, etc., followed by a Group Discussion and Personal Interview.

As of July 2025, the admission process for the 2025-26 session would likely be concluding. It is best to check the official SMS Varanasi website for the most current and accurate admission status and deadlines.

Is SMS Varanasi UGC approved?

UGC has granted SMS the Prestigious Autonomous status. This management college in Varanasi has been relentlessly pursuing the ruthless path of growth and excellence and has a growing recognition as one of the top B-schools in India.

What are the eligibility criteria for all courses at SMS Varanasi?
Undergraduate Courses (BBA, BCA, B.Com, B.Com (Hons), BA (Hons) in Mass Communication):
  • Must have passed 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board.
  • Minimum percentage and subject requirements may vary by course.
Postgraduate Courses:
  • MBA : Graduation in any discipline with a minimum of 50% marks (45% for SC/ST). Final-year students may apply.
  • MCA : Graduation in any discipline with Mathematics at 10+2 or graduation level, with a minimum of 50% marks (45% for SC/ST). Final-year students may apply.
