Chat Analytics

Our Chat Analytics module transforms raw chatbot conversations into actionable intelligence for the institution. It provides a comprehensive dashboard to monitor user engagement, track the most frequently asked questions, and measure the chatbot's performance in real-time. By understanding student queries related to admissions, fees, exams, and campus facilities, administrators can identify key trends, improve services, and make data-driven decisions to enhance the overall student experience and streamline administrative tasks.
