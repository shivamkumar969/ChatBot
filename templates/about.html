Introduction

This project aims to develop a simple web-based chatbot application using the Flask framework with AI & DS. The chatbot will serve as an interactive tool for new college students to get answers to common queries regarding college information, admissions, courses, facilities, etc. The primary goal is to provide instant and easily accessible information, reducing the burden on administrative staff and helping new students navigate their initial college experience smoothly. The chatbot will be designed with a user-friendly interface, allowing students to type their questions and receive relevant pre-defined responses.

The "Simple Chatbot for College Queries" is a an Al & DS based application that allows new or prospective students to interact with a virtual assistant (chatbot) and get answers to frequently asked questions related to the college.
